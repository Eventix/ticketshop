FAQ

The most questions our users have are answered below. Is your question not listed? Feel free to contact us and ask us your question in person.

I have not received an email after ordering tickets, what do I do now?

If you have not received your tickets withing half an hour after placing your order, ant here has been money deducted from your account, you can follow the steps below.

Step 1

Check your spam/junk folder. We do our best to make sure tickets are sent to the inbox, however sometimes people have strict spam-filters that place the mail containing the link to your tickets into the junk folder.

Step 2

If you have cookies enabled in your browser, you can go back to the page where you ordered the tickets to view the details of your order (see the image below). Click on 'status' (image 1) to view your order details. You will see the status of your order as displayed in image 2. It might be the case that you mistyped your email address and that this might be the reason your tickets have not arrived.

Step 3

If you do not have cookies enabled, you can find your status overview through https://eventix.io/search/search-tickets/ using your transaction-ID. The transaction-ID is a 16 digit number. An example of a transaction-ID is shown in the image below.

Do I have to print my ticket?

You may, but you do not have to. You can also display the QR code of a digital ticket from the screen of your phone. Make sure you have enough battery. It often happens that people appear at the entrance with an empty phone claiming they have a ticket but cannot show it because of a dead battery.

Where can I find tickets of an event of which Eventix facilitates the ticket sales?

On https://eventix.io/search/order-tickets/ you can search for the event you are looking for. Select an event and you will be redirected to the ticketshop.
